THE CHALLENGE

Subaru is large automaker best known for engineering, safety, reliability, and functionality of its vehicles. Subaru’s business model centered on selection and concentration of limited management resources, creating added value, and pursuing uncompromising differentiation

One of Subaru dealer’s goal was to understand return of investment from their marketing activities. Due to the nature of the business, the vast majority of purchases take place offline after the buyer has passed a test drive, and because of this, the company had difficulty assessing the quality of advertising campaigns.

Subaru tasked CAMPAIGNNAME to connect online and offline data into single source of truth for

Technologies

CAMPAIGNNAME started from enhancing Google Analytics setup with call and form tracking in order to understand which sessions lead to a test-drive. Since Subaru invests heavily in Google Ads display campaigns, it was crucial to understand post-view conversions, which is why parallel tracking was implemented in 360 Campaign Manager.

As real time data was not required, CAMPAIGNNAME set up batch data downloads from Google Analytics, Campaign Manager 360 and CRM to Google Cloud Platform, and joined test drive entries with vehicle purchases.

An automatic Data Studio report with key business and digital marketing metrics has been created to monitor campaign performance in real time. Sales funnel report was created as well , starting from calls and form submits, ending up with car or service sales with marketing channel breakdown.

THE RESULTS

CAMPAIGNNAME was able to successfully connect online and offline data. Further, these connection efforts resulted in a 41% increase in ROAS for Subaru's entire digital marketing stack.

Through CAMPAIGNNAME’s work, Subaru can better understand customer journey and feel confident in their campaigns performance.
